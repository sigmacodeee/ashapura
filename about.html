THE AC HALL

Ashapura Garden Restaurant's AC Hall – nestled on the Rajkot-Bhavnagar Highway in Sihor, offers a premium dining experience with a perfect blend of tradition and comfort. Our air-conditioned hall is designed for family gatherings, festive celebrations, and intimate dinners. The elegant interiors reflect the warmth of Gujarati culture while maintaining contemporary sophistication.

Every dish served from our kitchen carries the authentic taste of Kathiyawadi and Gujarati cuisine, made using time-honored recipes and the freshest local ingredients. Whether you're a traveller passing through Sihor or a local resident, the AC Hall provides a serene escape where flavors speak of heritage and hospitality.

OUR FOUNDER

KARSANBHAI B. RAJPUT

Visionary behind Ashapura Garden Restaurant, established in 1998 with a dream to bring authentic Kathiyawadi flavors to the heart of Sihor.
“My vision was to create a dining space where every guest feels like family — where the aroma of spices tells stories of our land, and every meal leaves a lasting memory. Ashapura is not just a restaurant; it's the continuation of a culinary heritage passed through generations.”
— Karsanbhai B. Rajput, Founder

Located strategically on the Rajkot-Bhavnagar Highway, our restaurant has become a landmark for travelers and food lovers seeking authentic regional cuisine. We uphold the highest standards of taste, hygiene, and warm service — values that define the Rajput family legacy.

Sihor's Finest on Rajkot–Bhavnagar Highway

Ashapura Garden Restaurant proudly serves as a culinary destination on the bustling Rajkot-Bhavnagar Highway, drawing locals and tourists alike. Our prime location in Sihor makes us the ideal stop for authentic Gujarati thalis, fresh snacks, and exquisite vegetarian delicacies. We’re committed to preserving the rich food culture of Saurashtra while delivering world-class hospitality.
